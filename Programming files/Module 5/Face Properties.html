Face Properties

        Like edges, faces have evaluator functions, topological properties, and geometric properties.

Face Topology Properties

        Like an edge, a face has “topological” properties that describe its relationship to other objects in its body. If myFace
        is an NXOpen.Face object, then myFace.GetBody gives you the body that the face lies on, and myFace.GetEdges returns its
        array of edges..
