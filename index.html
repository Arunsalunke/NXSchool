About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer and Automation Specialist with over 10 years of hands-on experience in NX Open Automation and C# .NET development.

Over the past decade, I have worked extensively on automating complex CAD workflows using Siemens NX, focusing on productivity improvement, design standardization, and high-performance automation solutions for real-world engineering problems.

My core expertise lies in building robust NX Open applications using C#, covering part modeling, assembly automation, drawing creation, PMI & GD&T extraction, geometry analysis, feature recognition, and large-scale batch processing.

What I Teach

  • NX Open fundamentals to advanced automation concepts
  • C# programming for Mechanical Engineers
  • Object-Oriented Programming with CAD examples
  • Design patterns used in automation projects
  • Performance optimization for large NX models
  • Industry-focused real-world automation projects

I strongly believe that learning automation should be practical, structured, and industry-oriented. My teaching approach focuses on:
